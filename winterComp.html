What is a pitch competition?

A pitch competition is an event where aspiring entrepreneurs present their business ideas to a panel of judges, competing for prizes or funding. Participants deliver short, impactful presentations of their business ideas that showcase their innovation, strategy, and potential for success.


Why should I join?
