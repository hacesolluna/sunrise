OUR TEAM

Ellen Wang

Founder & President

Ellen Wang is the Founder & President of the CA-wide Sunrise Venture Pitch Competition. As the President of Northwood High's Entrepreneurship Club, she was inspired to expand this pitch competition into a state-wide event. She has garnered a $500 grant from Irvine Mayor Farrah Khan and united the OC startup community–business professors, investors, and entrepreneurs–to create a free online curriculum and mentorship program for high school students. Her vision is to debunk the myth that entrepreneurs have to be silicon valley tech gurus and to make superb entrepreneurial education as well as networking opportunities accessible to all students regardless of economic privilege, because few people can afford $7000 entrepreneurship summer programs.

Maya Iyengar

Director of Outreach

Maya Iyengar is the director of outreach for the Sunrise Venture Pitch Competition. She is a junior at Northwood High School in Irvine, California. Maya is very passionate about tackling inequities in education, and joined Sunrise Venture to expand the horizons of career exploration and entrepreneurship education for high school students. Outside of Sunrise Venture, Maya is also a board member of the Entrepreneurship Club, Stock Market Club, and President of the Days for Girls club at her school, showcasing her passion for business and community service.

Shuba Senthilkumar

Curriculum and Mentorship Coordinator

Shuba Senthilkumar is the Curriculum and Mentorship Coordinator of Sunrise Venture Pitch Competition. Also outside of Sunrise, you can catch her serving her schools ASB, leading various service projects as the president of University High School's Key Club, or interning for startups and nonprofits– generally fostering a community, locally and regionally. Sunrise Venture Pitch Competition is one of her entrepreneurial nonprofits she focuses on enriching with her contributions. Shuba connects with the seasoned entrepreneurs, investors, and professors of California to invite to the program's weekly speaker sessions and mentorship sessions. She ensures every participant receives clear and cohesive information, accommodating their needs and catering to their wants tirelessly. Her vision is granting everyone equal and inclusive access to the resources, opportunities, and experiences, enabling everyone to rise and shine bright, regardless of any disadvantage they may have.
