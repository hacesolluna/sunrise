Sunrise Venture Pitch Competition

California-wide Entrepreneurship Competition for High School Students

Competition Format

1st round:

  • 90-second video pitch submission for all eligible participants due by April 1, 2024 11:59 pm PST
  • Finalists will be notified by April 10 and are eligible to compete in the 2nd round

2nd round:

  • 5-minute in-person pitches taking place in Irvine, California in early May
  • Date, location, and time TBD in December/January 2023

Prizes

🥇 1st Place: $3000 value

🥈 2nd Place: $2000 value

🥉 3rd Place: $1000 value

Composes of cash and opportunities such as...

  • Free consulting sessions with consultants
  • A dinner with a CEO
  • Internships
  • Memberships to entrepreneurship organizations and pitching events

Participant Eligibility

  • Be a U.S. Citizen or permanent resident
  • Live in California
  • Be currently enrolled in grades 9-12
  • Attend 5 speaker sessions and 1 mentorship session minimum
  • Compete as individuals or in teams of up to 3 people
