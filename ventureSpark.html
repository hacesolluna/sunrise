VENTURESPARK ACADEMY

WHAT IS IT?

VentureSpark Academy is a small, 5-session cohort program where driven, entrepreneurial students master the fundamentals of business, craft compelling pitches, and compete for up to $500 to start your company in the finale pitch competition--all for free! In our 5 bootcamps, you'll directly interact with successful business leaders, Harvard-grad entrepreneurs, and university professors.

WHY SHOULD I APPLY?

  • Exclusive access to pitch competition: win up to $500 to kickstart your business
  • Direct networking access to successful entrepreneurs
  • Explore entrepreneurship & business in exciting, hands-on, and practical way
  • Boost your college resume with your passion project!
