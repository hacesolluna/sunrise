ENTREPRENEURIAL EQUITY FOR SANTA ANA
Sign Up

This free 8-week program brings equitable entrepreneurship and financial literacy education to disadvantaged youth in Santa Ana, CA. Its speakers include alumni from UC Berkeley and Harvard, as well as industry experts in the medical technology and AI sectors, providing an unparalleled opportunity for youth to network and explore careers intersecting business and technology. Through presentations, workshops, discussions, and a mini pitch competition, teens will develop invaluable life skills.
