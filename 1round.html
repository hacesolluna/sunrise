THE RULES

  • All California high schoolers (Grades 9-12 currently) are eligible to submit a pitch
  • Submissions cannot be over 90 seconds
    • You will be disqualified if your pitch is over 90 seconds
  • Absolutely no edits, cuts, or music
    • Your video must be one take
    • If your video jump cuts or uses background music, you will be disqualified immediately
    • Exception: If you and your partner cannot film in-person together due to geographical constraints, you may stitch together your video pitch
      • It is also acceptable that you pitch in zoom and record it
  • You must follow the rubric to receive points
    • Any external influence or teachings will not be considered; we grade based on our rubric
  • You are no longer required to attend:
    • 5/10 curriculum sessions
    • 1 mentorship session
