About the Mentors

Mentors are successful, high-profile entrepreneurs who have donated their valuable time to provide pitch feedback to students

John Russo

John Russo is a full-time faculty at Irvine Valley College in the School of Business Sciences and is the faculty lead for the Entrepreneurship Program where he has hosted and judged multiple pitch competitions. He has more than 20 years of sales, management, and marketing experience in a variety of industries such as wireless technology, advertising, mortgage lending, and consumer and packaged goods. As an entrepreneur and business owner in the food industry, he successfully ran his own restaurant franchise and has an understanding of business ownership and entrepreneurial opportunities. He is currently a board member of Tech Coast Venture Network (TCVN), an entrepreneurship organization in Orange County.

Justin Sanders

Justin Sanders is an intellectual property attorney and managing partner at Master Key IP. For the past 17+ years, Justin has assisted clients (primarily startups and small businesses) with all aspects of identifying, protecting and monetizing their intellectual property in both the U.S. and abroad. Justin graduated from California State University, Long Beach, earning a B.S. in Computer Science with a minor in Human Resources Management. He went on to attend Whittier Law School as a Fellow in the Center for Intellectual Property Law, where he received his J.D. with magna cum laude honors along with a Certificate in Intellectual Property Law. In addition to servicing clients through Master Key IP, Justin is actively involved with a number of local universities and startup organizations, where he serves as a resource and mentor to resident entrepreneurs, providing guidance on various intellectual property issues.

Shaun Sanders

Shaun is the Managing Partner of VentureCraft and leverages his 20+ years of experience in the startup world to help early-stage companies commercialize their technology and raise capital. His clients have raised more than $400 million to date. Shaun also serves as the Senior Associate Director of one of the top SBDC-funded accelerators in the country, Octane LaunchPad. Additionally, Shaun is an Adjunct Professor of Law at USC Gould teaching business principles of law to attorneys and Masters' level students.

Vincent Moiso

Vincent Moiso is a principal and President of Oceanside Glass & Tile. He has been in the tile and flooring industry for 25 years with an international background in distribution and manufacturing. Vincent is also the founder and president of his own consulting and coaching practice focused primarily on small businesses. He has consulted for dozens of businesses advising on everything from organizational structure, strategic planning, to scaling and exiting a business. Vincent published a new on-line course in 2019, the Complete Guide on Developing Tile & Stone Specifications with Architects in partnership with Ceramic Tile & Stone Consultants. He also recently published his book, How to Survive the Wilderness: The Playbook for the Entrepreneur and High Performer, which has been a top seller in several categories on Amazon. Vincent is a certified DiSC and Lead With Purpose trainer, both tools that are emphasized in all of his coaching engagements. With an MBA from Cal State Fullerton and a BA in international relations from the University of San Diego, Vincent has also taught a business course for a decade at his alma mater where he serves as President of the Alumni Board of Directors for the University.
